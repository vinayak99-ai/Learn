# English Quiz for Grade 7

Welcome to the Grade 7 English Quiz! This quiz contains 10 questions designed to test your knowledge of English vocabulary, grammar, synonyms, antonyms, and more. Each question has four answer choices (A, B, C, D). Take your time and choose the best answer for each question.

---

### Question 1
**Topic:** Vocabulary

What does the word "benevolent" mean?

A) Harmful  
B) Kind and generous  
C) Angry  
D) Confused  

**Correct Answer:** B) Kind and generous

---

### Question 2
**Topic:** Synonyms

Which word is a synonym for "abundant"?

A) Scarce  
B) Empty  
C) Plentiful  
D) Minimal  

**Correct Answer:** C) Plentiful

---

### Question 3
**Topic:** Antonyms

What is the antonym of "ancient"?

A) Modern  
B) Old  
C) Historic  
D) Traditional  

**Correct Answer:** A) Modern

---

### Question 4
**Topic:** Spelling

Which sentence has the correct spelling?

A) The committee will meet tommorrow.  
B) The committee will meet tomorrow.  
C) The comittee will meet tomorrow.  
D) The committee will meat tomorrow.  

**Correct Answer:** B) The committee will meet tomorrow.

---

### Question 5
**Topic:** Grammar (Parts of Speech)

In the sentence "The quick brown fox jumped over the lazy dog," what part of speech is the word "quick"?

A) Noun  
B) Verb  
C) Adjective  
D) Adverb  

**Correct Answer:** C) Adjective

---

### Question 6
**Topic:** Vocabulary

What does the word "reluctant" mean?

A) Eager and willing  
B) Hesitant or unwilling  
C) Confused  
D) Happy  

**Correct Answer:** B) Hesitant or unwilling

---

### Question 7
**Topic:** Context Clues

In the sentence "The arid desert had not seen rain for months," what does "arid" most likely mean?

A) Wet  
B) Cold  
C) Dry  
D) Mountainous  

**Correct Answer:** C) Dry

---

### Question 8
**Topic:** Synonyms

Which word is a synonym for "diligent"?

A) Lazy  
B) Careless  
C) Hardworking  
D) Slow  

**Correct Answer:** C) Hardworking

---

### Question 9
**Topic:** Antonyms

What is the antonym of "courageous"?

A) Brave  
B) Bold  
C) Fearless  
D) Cowardly  

**Correct Answer:** D) Cowardly

### Question 10
**Topic:** Grammar (Parts of Speech)

In the sentence "She sang beautifully at the concert," what part of speech is the word "beautifully"?

B) Adverb
